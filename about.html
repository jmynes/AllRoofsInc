Welcome! Read a little about us.

A roof is perhaps the most integral part of a home. The three basic human needs include shelter, food, and water. We speak of "the roof over our heads" that we hope to provide for our families. A roof holds together the walls, and protects the people and possessions that make a house a home.

The founder and owner of All Roofs, Inc - a local Chicago roofing company - strongly agrees with this idea.

Starting out as a roofer himself, Dusan had a vision. He wanted to take his high personal standards and love for roofing, and turn those things into a company that could provide customers with products and installation services that were up to those standards. A father of three, Dusan knew when he started All Roofs, Inc. over 15 years ago that he wanted to base his business around quality work - and prices that the average homeowner or business owner could afford.

All Roofs Inc. is a multi-trade company that specializes in all types of roofing and residential or commercial exterior trades. Dusan and the rest of his team strive to provide their customers with the highest quality customer service and work available in the Chicagoland area. Unlike most other roofing contractors, All Roofs maintains several of their own in-house roofing crews.

This allows All Roofs to be more involved in every step of the roofing process, and helps ensure top-notch results, every time. Utilizing knowledge and skills from his roots as a roofer, Dusan makes sure to be at every commercial job All Roofs is doing. He personally inspects the job his crews are doing, so he can guarantee the customer is receiving the highest-quality installation of products.

If you're looking for affordability and quality when it comes to choosing a contractor for your roofing or exterior projects, All Roofs, Inc. will meet your needs and more.
